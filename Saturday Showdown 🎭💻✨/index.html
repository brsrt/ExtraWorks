Weekly Representative Activities

Description

Every Saturday, BCA students are invited to participate in an engaging and informative session lasting 2 hours. Held in the College Seminar Room or Auditorium, these sessions are designed to enhance learning and foster individual development. The schedule ensures a consistent and structured environment, with each week offering a unique opportunity for growth and interaction. Join us every Saturday to take advantage of this valuable experience tailored specifically for BCA students.
